# Enterprise Onboarding Guide

Welcome to the organization. This guide covers essential policies and procedures.

## Remote Work Policy

All employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Manager approval is required for full remote arrangements.

## Expense Reimbursement

Submit expenses within 30 days via the finance portal. Receipts are required for amounts over $25.

## IT Security

- Enable MFA on all accounts
- Do not share credentials
- Report security incidents to security@company.com within 1 hour

## PTO Policy

Employees accrue 15 days PTO annually. Submit requests at least 2 weeks in advance for planned time off.
